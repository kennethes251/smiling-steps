## ğŸ†˜ When Things Go Wrong

### Quick Fixes (Try These First)

**Can't See "Join Call" Button?**
1. Refresh your dashboard page
2. Check if it's within 15 minutes of session time
3. Verify your payment is confirmed

**Camera Not Working?**
1. Click the camera icon in your browser's address bar
2. Select "Allow" for camera
3. Refresh the page

**Can't Hear Your Therapist?**
1. Check your computer's volume
2. Try using headphones
3. Click the microphone icon in your browser's address bar and allow

**Connection Problems?**
1. Move closer to your WiFi router
2. Close other internet-using apps (Netflix, YouTube)
3. Try refreshing the page

### Emergency Backup Plan

If video completely fails:
1. **Call your therapist directly** (phone number in session details)
2. **Use the chat feature** on your dashboard
3. **Email support**: support@smilingsteps.com with "URGENT" in subject
4. **Don't worry** - your session can continue by phone or be rescheduled

## â“ Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What if I'm not comfortable being on video?**
A: You can turn off your camera and do audio-only. Your therapist will understand.

**Q: Can my family members join the call?**
A: Only with your therapist's permission and your explicit consent. Sessions are typically one-on-one.

**Q: What if my internet is slow?**
A: Turn off your video to use audio-only mode. This uses much less internet.

**Q: Do I need to dress up for video sessions?**
A: Dress comfortably as you would for an in-person session. Your therapist wants you to feel relaxed.

**Q: What if I accidentally end the call?**
A: Just click "Join Call" again. Your therapist will be waiting, and your session time continues.

**Q: Can I eat or drink during the video session?**
A: Yes, just like in-person therapy. Having water is especially recommended.
