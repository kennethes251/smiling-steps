## Quick Solutions for Common Issues

### üö® Can't Join Video Call

**Symptoms:**
- "Join Call" button doesn't appear
- Button appears but clicking does nothing
- Error message: "Cannot join call at this time"

**Solutions:**
1. **Check Session Timing**
   - Button appears 15 minutes before session
   - Available up to 2 hours after scheduled time
   - Refresh your dashboard if time has passed

2. **Verify Payment Status**
   - Payment must be confirmed before joining
   - Check your session details for payment status
   - Contact support if payment issues persist

3. **Clear Browser Cache**
   - Press `Ctrl+Shift+Delete` (Windows) or `Cmd+Shift+Delete` (Mac)
   - Select "Cached images and files"
   - Clear and refresh the page

---

### üìπ Camera/Video Issues

**Symptoms:**
- Black screen instead of video
- "Camera permission denied" error
- Can't see yourself or other participant

**Solutions:**
1. **Grant Camera Permissions**
   - Click the camera icon in your browser's address bar
   - Select "Allow" for camera access
   - Refresh the page after granting permissions

2. **Check Camera Availability**
   - Close other video apps (Zoom, Teams, Skype)
   - Unplug and reconnect external cameras
   - Try a different browser (Chrome recommended)

3. **Test Camera in System Settings**
   - Windows: Settings > Privacy > Camera
   - Mac: System Preferences > Security & Privacy > Camera
   - Ensure your browser has camera access

**Browser-Specific Instructions:**

**Chrome:**
1. Click the lock icon next to the URL
2. Set Camera to "Allow"
3. Refresh the page

**Firefox:**
1. Click the shield icon in the address bar
2. Turn off "Enhanced Tracking Protection" for this site
3. Refresh and allow camera when prompted

**Safari:**
1. Safari menu > Preferences > Websites > Camera
2. Set to "Allow" for this website
3. Refresh the page

### üé§ Audio/Microphone Issues

**Symptoms:**
- Can't hear other participant
- Other participant can't hear you
- Echo or feedback during call

**Solutions:**
1. **Check Microphone Permissions**
   - Follow same steps as camera permissions above
   - Ensure microphone is selected in browser settings

2. **Fix Audio Issues**
   - Use headphones to prevent echo
   - Check system volume levels
   - Test microphone in system settings

3. **Reduce Echo/Feedback**
   - Use headphones instead of speakers
   - Move away from your microphone
   - Ask other participant to mute when not speaking

---

### üåê Connection Problems

**Symptoms:**
- "Connecting..." message doesn't go away
- Video freezes or is choppy
- "Connection failed" error

**Solutions:**
1. **Check Internet Speed**
   - Minimum required: 1 Mbps upload/download
   - Test at [speedtest.net](https://speedtest.net)
   - Move closer to WiFi router

2. **Improve Connection**
   - Use wired ethernet instead of WiFi
   - Close bandwidth-heavy apps (streaming, downloads)
   - Restart your router if needed

3. **Browser Troubleshooting**
   - Try incognito/private browsing mode
   - Disable browser extensions temporarily
   - Clear browser cache and cookies

---

### üñ•Ô∏è Screen Sharing Issues

**Symptoms:**
- Screen share button doesn't work
- Other participant can't see shared screen
- Screen share stops unexpectedly

**Solutions:**
1. **Browser Compatibility**
   - Chrome/Edge: Full support
   - Firefox: Good support
   - Safari: Limited support (try Chrome instead)

2. **Permission Issues**
   - Allow screen recording permissions when prompted
   - Mac: System Preferences > Security & Privacy > Screen Recording
   - Windows: Usually no additional permissions needed

3. **Performance Issues**
   - Close unnecessary applications
   - Share specific application instead of entire screen
   - Reduce screen resolution if needed

## Step-by-Step Connection Guide

### For First-Time Users:

1. **Before Your Session (5 minutes early)**
   - Close other video applications
   - Test your camera and microphone
   - Ensure stable internet connection
   - Use Chrome browser for best experience

2. **Joining the Call**
   - Log into your dashboard
   - Look for "Join Call" button (appears 15 min before)
   - Click the button
   - Allow camera and microphone when prompted
   - Wait for connection (usually 5-10 seconds)

3. **During the Call**
   - Your video appears in small box (top-right)
   - Other participant's video fills the main screen
   - Use controls at bottom to mute/unmute, turn video on/off
   - Click "End Call" when finished

4. **If Something Goes Wrong**
   - Try refreshing the page
   - Check your internet connection
   - Ensure camera/microphone permissions are granted
   - Contact support if issues persist

## Advanced Troubleshooting

### Network Configuration Issues

**Corporate/School Networks:**
- May block WebRTC traffic
- Contact IT department about firewall settings
- Try using mobile hotspot as alternative

**Router/Firewall Issues:**
- Ensure UDP traffic is allowed
- Check if UPnP is enabled on router
- Try connecting from different network to test

### Hardware-Specific Issues

**External Cameras:**
- USB cameras: Try different USB port
- Wireless cameras: Check battery and connection
- Multiple cameras: Select correct one in browser settings

**Audio Devices:**
- Bluetooth headphones: May have delay, use wired if possible
- USB headsets: Ensure drivers are installed
- Built-in microphones: May pick up keyboard noise

### Performance Optimization

**For Slower Computers:**
- Close unnecessary browser tabs
- Close other applications
- Reduce video quality by turning off camera temporarily
- Use audio-only mode if video is too choppy

**For Slow Internet:**
- Turn off video, use audio-only
- Close other internet-using applications
- Ask others in household to pause streaming/downloads
- Consider upgrading internet plan

## Error Messages Explained

### "Camera permission denied"
**Cause:** Browser blocked camera access  
**Fix:** Click camera icon in address bar, select "Allow"

### "Microphone permission denied"
**Cause:** Browser blocked microphone access  
**Fix:** Click microphone icon in address bar, select "Allow"

### "Connection failed"
**Cause:** Network or firewall issues  
**Fix:** Check internet connection, try different network

### "Session not found"
**Cause:** Invalid session or expired link  
**Fix:** Return to dashboard, use correct "Join Call" button

### "Payment not confirmed"
**Cause:** Session payment hasn't been processed  
**Fix:** Complete payment or contact support

### "Cannot join call at this time"
**Cause:** Outside allowed time window  
**Fix:** Wait until 15 minutes before session time
