## Frequently Asked Questions

### Getting Started

**Q: What do I need for video calls?**
A: You need:
- A computer or tablet with camera and microphone
- Chrome browser (recommended) or Firefox/Safari
- Stable internet connection (1+ Mbps)
- Confirmed payment for your session

**Q: Do I need to download any software?**
A: No! Video calls work directly in your web browser. No downloads required.

**Q: Which browsers work best?**
A: Chrome and Microsoft Edge work best. Firefox and Safari have basic support. Avoid Internet Explorer.

**Q: Can I use my phone for video calls?**
A: Mobile browsers have limited support. We recommend using a computer or tablet for the best experience.

---

### Before Your Session

**Q: When can I join my video call?**
A: The "Join Call" button appears 15 minutes before your scheduled session time. You can join up to 2 hours after the scheduled time.

**Q: Why don't I see a "Join Call" button?**
A: Common reasons:
- Session is more than 15 minutes away
- Payment hasn't been confirmed
- Session was cancelled or declined
- You're not logged into the correct account

**Q: How do I test my camera and microphone?**
A: Visit your computer's system settings:
- Windows: Settings > Privacy > Camera/Microphone
- Mac: System Preferences > Security & Privacy > Camera/Microphone

**Q: Should I close other video apps?**
A: Yes! Close Zoom, Teams, Skype, and other video applications before joining your call.

---

### During Your Session

**Q: How do I know if my video call is working?**
A: You should see:
- Your own video in a small box (usually top-right)
- Your therapist's video in the main screen
- A timer showing call duration
- Control buttons at the bottom

**Q: Can I turn off my camera during the call?**
A: Yes, click the camera button to turn your video on or off. Your therapist will see a black screen when your camera is off.

**Q: How do I mute myself?**
A: Click the microphone button to mute/unmute. When muted, your therapist won't hear you.

**Q: Can I share my screen?**
A: Yes, click the screen share button. This lets you show documents or websites to your therapist.

**Q: What if I can't see or hear my therapist?**
A: Try these steps:
1. Wait 10-15 seconds for connection
2. Refresh your browser page
3. Check your internet connection
4. Contact support if issues persist

---

### Technical Issues

**Q: I get a "permission denied" error. What do I do?**
A: Your browser needs permission to use your camera and microphone:
1. Look for a camera icon in your browser's address bar
2. Click it and select "Allow"
3. Refresh the page and try again

**Q: My video is choppy or freezes. How can I fix this?**
A: This usually means slow internet:
- Move closer to your WiFi router
- Close other internet-using apps
- Try turning off your video (audio-only)
- Use an ethernet cable if possible

**Q: I can see myself but not my therapist. What's wrong?**
A: This can happen if:
- Your therapist hasn't joined yet (wait a few minutes)
- Connection is still establishing (wait 10-15 seconds)
- Network issues (try refreshing the page)

**Q: There's an echo during our call. How do I stop it?**
A: Use headphones! This prevents your microphone from picking up sound from your speakers.

**Q: The video call page won't load. What should I do?**
A: Try these steps in order:
1. Refresh the page
2. Clear your browser cache
3. Try an incognito/private window
4. Try a different browser (Chrome recommended)
5. Restart your computer

### Payment and Scheduling

**Q: Do I need to pay before joining a video call?**
A: Yes, payment must be confirmed before the "Join Call" button appears.

**Q: What if my payment is processing during my session time?**
A: Contact support immediately. We can manually confirm your session while payment processes.

**Q: Can I reschedule if I have technical problems?**
A: Yes, if technical issues prevent your session, we can reschedule at no extra charge.

**Q: What happens if my therapist can't join the video call?**
A: Your therapist will contact you directly (phone or chat) to arrange an alternative or reschedule.

### Privacy and Security

**Q: Are video calls recorded?**
A: No, we do not record video calls. Your session is private between you and your therapist.

**Q: Is my video call secure?**
A: Yes, all video calls use end-to-end encryption. Only you and your therapist can see and hear the session.

**Q: Can other people join my video call?**
A: No, only you and your assigned therapist can join. Each session has a unique, secure room.

**Q: What information is stored about my video calls?**
A: We only store:
- Call duration (for billing)
- Connection quality metrics (for improvement)
- No video, audio, or conversation content

### Troubleshooting by Browser

### Chrome (Recommended)
**Q: Chrome says "Camera blocked" - how do I fix this?**
A: 
1. Click the camera icon in the address bar
2. Select "Allow" for camera and microphone
3. Refresh the page

**Q: Chrome is slow during video calls. What can I do?**
A: 
- Close other Chrome tabs
- Disable unnecessary extensions
- Clear browsing data (Ctrl+Shift+Delete)

### Firefox
**Q: Firefox won't let me share my screen. Why?**
A: Screen sharing support in Firefox is limited. Try Chrome for screen sharing.

**Q: I get tracking protection warnings in Firefox. What should I do?**
A: Click the shield icon in the address bar and turn off Enhanced Tracking Protection for our site.

### Safari
**Q: Video calls don't work well in Safari. What's wrong?**
A: Safari has limited WebRTC support. We recommend switching to Chrome for the best experience.

**Q: Safari keeps asking for camera permission. How do I make it remember?**
A: Go to Safari > Preferences > Websites > Camera and set our site to "Allow".

**Q: What about Android phones?**

### Emergency Situations

**Q: What if video calls completely fail during my session?**
A: Backup options:
1. Call your therapist directly (phone number in session details)
2. Use the chat feature on your dashboard
3. Contact support immediately: support@smilingsteps.com
4. Session can be rescheduled if needed

**Q: My session starts in 2 minutes and video won't work. What do I do?**
A: 
1. Try refreshing the page quickly
2. Call your therapist's direct number immediately
3. Email support@smilingsteps.com with "URGENT" in subject
4. Don't worry - we'll make sure you get your session

**Q: What if my internet goes out during the session?**
A: 
- Try reconnecting when internet returns
- Call your therapist if you have phone service
- Session time will be extended to make up for lost time

### Best Practices

**Q: How can I ensure the best video call experience?**
A: Follow this checklist:
- Use Chrome browser
- Close other video applications
- Use headphones to prevent echo
- Ensure good lighting (face a window if possible)
- Test your setup 5 minutes before the session
- Have a backup plan (phone number) ready

**Q: What's the best internet speed for video calls?**
A: Minimum 1 Mbps upload/download. For HD video, 5+ Mbps is recommended.

**Q: Should I use WiFi or ethernet?**
A: Ethernet (wired) connection is more stable, but good WiFi works fine too.
