# Smiling Steps - Product Overview

Smiling Steps is a mental health teletherapy platform connecting clients with licensed psychologists for online therapy sessions.

## Core Features

- **Client Registration & Verification**: Email-verified client accounts with intake forms
- **Psychologist Management**: Admin-approved psychologist profiles with availability scheduling
- **Session Booking**: Clients book therapy sessions with available psychologists
- **Video Calls**: WebRTC-based video therapy sessions with screen sharing
- **Payments**: M-Pesa integration for Kenya market, with payment reconciliation
- **Assessments**: Mental health assessments and progress tracking
- **Blog & Resources**: Educational content and downloadable resources
- **Admin Dashboard**: User management, payment oversight, and analytics

## User Roles

1. **Client**: Books sessions, takes assessments, attends video calls
2. **Psychologist**: Manages availability, conducts sessions, views earnings
3. **Admin**: Manages users, approves psychologists, oversees payments

## Target Market

Primary focus on Kenya with M-Pesa payment integration. Platform supports both local development and Render deployment.
